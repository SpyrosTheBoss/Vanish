Vanish removes objects and watermarks from your photos, entirely on your device.

Tap the thing you want gone and an AI segmentation model outlines it exactly; an inpainting model then rebuilds the background behind it. Lasso and brush tools are there when you want manual control.

Nothing is uploaded — all detection and inpainting runs locally.

Features:

* Tap-to-select powered by MobileSAM
* On-device inpainting with MI-GAN (bundled, fast)
* Optional higher-quality LaMa model, downloaded on demand
* Lasso and brush masking, with undo/redo
* Before/after comparison slider
* Save to gallery and share
* Material 3 design with dynamic color (Material You) and a themed icon

Runs fully offline. The only network use is the optional one-time download of the LaMa model if you choose it in Settings.
